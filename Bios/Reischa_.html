Reischa is an experienced Belly Dance Performer, Choreo-
grapher, and Instructor focused on both traditional and modern fusion styles. She has been a dancer and choreo-
grapher for world fusion group Labyrinthe since 1999, and later founded The Labyrinthe Belly Dance Company. Reischa received her degree in Theater Performance from MTSU, where she served as choreographer for the university's production of Tales of Arabian Nights.

Her performances include an impressive list of festivals, Arabic gatherings, and restaurants. She currently teaches
at Rising Sun Karate & Dance (Gallatin, TN), Centennial Arts Center, and Vanderbilt University. (Nashville, TN)

Along with a strong background in theater performance, Reischa brings to this art form training in Ballet, Jazz, and Flamenco. She has studied Middle Eastern Dance with accomplished Arabic musicians and dancers such as Laylia White, Aziza and Suhaila Salimpour.
