BELLY DANCE:
Clothing - Be comfortable and able to move freely. I recommend jazz pants, exercise pants, cropped, fitted dance tops, or leotards. A hip scarf without coins is preferred for class work.
Shoes - Not required, however you may prefer to wear jazz or lyrical shoes and some street shoes may work for class.
Discouraged - Please do not wear jeans, non-dance skirts, street shoes, & other restricting articles.

SPANISH DANCE:
Clothing - For most classes, any modest exercise or dance wear is suitable for most classes. Close to exams a black flamenco skirt, tights, and a black long-sleeved leotard is required.
Shoes - Character shoes and some dress shoes are ok to start. Flamenco shoes are preferred, and will be most suitable for exams & performance.
Discouraged - Please do not wear jeans, non-dance skirts, street shoes, & other restricting articles.
