Reischa is a Professional Belly Dance Artist in Nashville, TN. A dedicated performer and teacher, she is internationally praised for her artistic and authentic representation of Raks Sharki. Her enthusiastic performances span many forms but reflect a strong focus on Egyptian Classical and Folkloric stylization.

In addition to devoting her life to this dance form, she continues to keep up her professional work and training in acting and music.

More on the artist...
- Served as Belly Dance instructor at Vanderbilt University for 12 years.
- A most sought after Belly Dancer within Nashville's Middle Eastern Community since 2004.
- Guest dancer at many weddings, engagement parties, and other special events.
